Phones

There is much to say about mobile phones like how portable they are and how consistent they are, but in my opinion the cons completely outweigh the pros

Specifications

everything technological has specification it's the parts inside of it that make it work.

Take the Iphone 15 for example. The newest Iphone, (at the time of writing this.) the parts inside this are as follows

  • Capacity - between 128GB - 256GB - 512GB
  • 6-core CPU with 2 performance and 4 efficiency cores
  • 5-core GPU
  • 16-core Neural engine
  • A16 bionic chip

Overall

all the specs are pretty good for a mobile phone yet it still has major issues like overheating and horrible durability and remember we are trying for something that last 8 years minimum and I dont think this is gonna last more than a year. This isnt all though as Iphones get outdated and become irrelevant really easily.
