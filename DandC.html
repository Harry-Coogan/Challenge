Consoles and Laptops

Consoles

Consoles. usually the first thing people buy if they try to get into gaming for good reasons the most companies that own the consoles take care and are pretty understanding to their community. but other than game they cant really do too much.

Specifications

Let's take the Xbox Series X and have a look at it's specs.

  • CPU: 8x Zen 2 Cores at 3.8GHz (3.6GHz with SMT)
  • GPU: 12 TFLOPs, 52 CUs at 1.825GHz, Custom RDNA 2
  • Die Size: 360.45mm2
  • Process: TSMC 7nm Enhanced
  • Memory: 16GB GDDR6
  • Memory Bandwidth: 10GB at 560GB/s, 6GB at 336GB/s
  • Internal Storage: 1TB Custom NVMe SSD
  • I/O Throughput: 2.4GB/s (Raw), 4.8GB/s (Compressed)
  • External Storage: USB 3.2 HDD Support
  • G-Story Cooling Fan

Overall

These specs are very good especially with the adaptible cooling system, but even with all of these amazing features theres nothing more you can do other than play games. The Xbox series X may well last 8 years but definitely not in good condition after the fact.

Laptops

Laptops are a pretty good option for daily use and can be used for lots of things like work and researching. Laptops can also be used for gaming but are nowhere near as good as as others mostly because they have a high chance of overheating because of how compact they are and what they can run.

Let's take the Surface Laptop 5 and take a look at it's specs.

  • 12th Gen Intel® Core™ i5/i7 processors
  • Intel® Evo™ platform
  • Lightning-fast Thunderbolt™ 4
  • 4k monitor
  • Intel® Iris® Xe Graphics
  • 8GB, 16GB LPDDR5x RAM
  • Removable solid-state drive (SSD) options: 256GB, 512GB

Overall

Will it last 8 years though? in an honest answer probably not depending on how well you take care of it things can easily get stuck inside the laptop and cause a complete malfunction.
